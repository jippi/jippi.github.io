Slides from my talks at CakeFest 2013

The slides for my 3 talks at CakeFest 2013 are now online

Scaling CakePHP

A talk on scaling CakePHP. It got surprisingly little to do with CakePHP, and so much more to do with PHP and the underlying infrastructure for your servers.

Crud - Application Development on steroids

Talk on CRUD, describing the pro and cons of bake, scaffolding and CRUD, and how awesome CRUD really is.

Friends Of Cake

I presented the vision for Friends Of Cake and what we want to achieve with the project and how it can benefit the CakePHP community.
